The Anatomy of a Secure Factory

We don't just sell software or hardware. We deliver a vertically integrated stack designed to survive the plant floor while satisfying the boardroom.

01

The Rugged Network

Physical Infrastructure & Switching

The foundation of the network must be as tough as the machines it connects. We replace fragile office gear with hardened industrial equipment.

  • Turnkey Enclosures: Pre-wired NEMA cabinets with DIN-rail mounting, power conditioning, and fiber termination.
  • High-Power PoE: 90W (4PPoE) per port to drive power-hungry Edge AI devices, computer vision cameras, and high-performance wireless APs without auxiliary power.
  • Rapid Recovery Architecture: Dual-homed uplinks eliminate single points of failure. We utilize local SD card backups for instant "Swap-and-Boot" replacementâ€”no laptop or IT support required to restore a failed switch.

02

Industrial Edge Compute

MDF Hyperconverged Infrastructure

Low latency and data sovereignty are critical. We deploy a private compute cluster directly to your MDF to host the intelligence stack at the edge.

  • Data Sovereignty: Critical security logs and access policies are stored locally, not in the public cloud.
  • High Availability: A 3-node cluster ensures that if one server fails, your security operations continue without a blip.
  • Air-Gap Ready: Designed to operate fully autonomously even if the internet connection is severed.

03

The Security Fabric

Identity & Threat Detection

We move beyond "Air Gaps" (which rarely work) to a modern Zero-Trust architecture that assumes the network is already breached.

  • Micro-Segmentation: We create virtual walls around every machine. A corrupted HMI cannot infect the PLC next to it.
  • Behavioral Analytics: AI that learns "normal" traffic. If a thermostat starts scanning the network, we kill the port instantly.
  • Identity Services: Every device must prove who it is (MAB/802.1X) before sending a single packet.

04

Operational Visibility

The "Single Pane of Glass"

Security tools are useless if Operations can't understand them. We translate complex network data into a language Plant Managers speak.

  • Asset Discovery: Automatically map every PLC, Drive, and IO Block. No more manual spreadsheets.
  • Vulnerability Matching: We correlate your exact hardware firmware against known CVEs to prioritize patching.
  • Process Context: See network flows in the context of the production line (e.g., "Line 1 / Mixing / PLC-04").
